Case Management and Analysis

Introducing a revolutionary case management and analysis platform for lawyers. Everchron brings together all of the information you need in a collaborative workspace for your entire team. And it does it in a way that's fast, effective, and intuitive.

Master the facts of your case

Everchron creates a timeline of a case's key documents and data, drawing intelligent connections to witness testimony, facts and issues that reflect and reveal critical information about the case.

Real-time client access and input

Clients no longer need to wait on and pay for outside counsel to prepare status updates or send the latest documents. With Everchron’s smart permission controls, clients have up-to-date dashboard access to the latest documents and filings for all their cases.

Connect everyone in one workspace

Case teams are often disconnected. Everchron provides a collaborative workspace for each case, giving you full control over who gets access - partners, associates or paralegals at a firm or even co-counsel, experts and clients.

  • Document Review Database
  • Docket Software
  • Document Management

Going beyond e-discovery

Everchron helps your team organize, analyze and discuss key information throughout the life of a case, from the complaint to depositions, motions, trial and appeals.

  • Complaint

Find everything you need, when you need it

With Everchron, all your case critical information is stored in a single, secure platform and can be accessed at any time, anywhere and from any device. So when it comes time to prepare for a deposition or hearing, everything you need is always at your fingertips.

  • Trial mindset from the beginning of a case
  • Improved client communication and access
  • Increased efficiency
  • Better internal communication
