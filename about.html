Legal software for today’s lawyer.

We offer a platform that lets you work with information as a team – mark it up, comment on it, discuss it, analyze it, draw connections and reveal the story of documents – and, ultimately, helps you win cases.

It was built by lawyers, for lawyers.

We understand all of the little things that go into how lawyers practice on a day-to-day basis.

It’s seriously fast.

Time is precious, especially for lawyers. We are making the path from input to results as quick and simple as possible.

It’s built for teams.

Everchron brings everyone together in a way that’s collaborative when it needs to be and private when it doesn’t.
